The HUMAN race has been increasingly overloading the atsmophere with carbon dioxide and this over time has created a blanket of heat. This is called a Carbon footprint and everyone has one. In addition, to everyone's carbon footprint this blaket is holding all the everyday green house gases from fossil fuels such as modern day oils, fuel gas,and even something as simple as hair-spray.
You may ask yourself how you could help the enviroment and make your carbon foot print smaller? First let's introduce the true definition of a carbon footprint. "The amount of carbon dioxide and other carbon compounds emitted due to consumption of fossil fuels by an indiviual" is the full definition. For example, how much water you use, if you recycle, electircity used, the amount of waste you produce and transport is considered when looking into your individual footprint.
Some easy solutions are to become more aware of how much of the enviroments resources you are using everyday. Heating & electricity conservation by only using what your need when you need it, using less water, eating less meat and more organic foods, and driving less or switching to an Eco-friendly vehicle are great ways to start!
